Intro to Dermot Kennedy

Biography

Dermot Joseph Kennedy is an Irish singer-songwriter. He is best known for his 2019 single "Outnumbered" and his 2020 single "Giants". He is signed to Interscrope Records in the US, and Island Records for the rest of the world. His debut major-label studio album, Without Fear, was released on 4 October 2019.
